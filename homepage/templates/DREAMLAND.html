MIRROR 2020

'Mirror' is an optional replacement of a more traditional mirror. It can be used as a reminder to practice, and subsequently present, self-love and authenticity. The blanket wrapped around the woman is inspired by ‘The Kiss’ by the Austrian Symbolist painter Gustav Klimt, to represent self-love. A naked body is chosen to represent embracing authenticity - being unapologetic of one's identity, including the quirks.

PEOPLE&PLACES: LONDON


A selection of line drawings from Jess' People&Places: London edition, printed and placed on cards for you to share with others.

- Print framed in a 6 by 4 inches, 380gsm, cream card.

- The inside is left blank, for you to add your own message. Add message at checkout to send direct, or leave blank and send to yourself with a spare envelope.

- Printed on demand to reduce waste.

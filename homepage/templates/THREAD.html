THREAD

Embroidered photographs


I really enjoy the process of being able to collaborate with others through photograph embroidery, as some photographs used where not captured by myself. This increased the depth and sentimental meaning to the work.

For example, I was able to collaborate with my late grandfather whilst working on photographs he had taken, as I joined together my embroidery and the imagery that he had seen, experienced and chosen to capture. I treasure this technique for its ability to connect me with others that I love.

JUST JUMP

An exploration of courage


We all live within our own set of paradigms, and have points of reassessment and change.

Stepping into the unknown can be unsettling. We often need to practice faith in our instincts when choosing a new direction in our lives.

The final piece took inspiration from the thought of explorers who wanted to discover if the world was indeed flat - specifically when they courageously reached the end of their knowledge of the earth, and found that their world did not end but expanded. The same may be true on a more personal level - we search for answers to questions, we fear, take courage, and go seek knowledge or life experience, and although it can be challenging we find our world doesn't end where it used to, it expands.

I kept the imagery very simple, taking inspiration from creating work in as few lines as possible whilst still conveying the message.

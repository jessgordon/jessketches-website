C19

Sketches, photographs, paintings and animations created throughout the COVID-19 pandemic (or relevant to this experience)

A state of flux: gratitude for provisions to ‘stay safe’ working from home, yet fear of how long it will last; appreciation for music, art, companionship, time to just be, but frustration of grieving without each other, without embrace; peace, loss.

PROTECTION & PREVENTION 2020

I was playing with light and shadows created by a front facing window in my home. Pen on paper was used to dot a symbol of COVID 19 floating around outside. There are mixed emotions for the bar like shadows created by the net curtains; gratitude for protecting us from the virus, yet frustration for holding us back from embracing those we love.

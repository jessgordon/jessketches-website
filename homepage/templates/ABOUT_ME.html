Hi!

I'm Jess and I love to create. It's my happy place.

I gravitate towards line-drawings using black ink, shaded pencil drawings, acrylic or oil paintings, and collage. The human figure presents itself in a lot of my work, as it's a great platform to explore lived experience. I enjoy focusing on the unglorified moments in life, celebrating it as the space where we spend most of our time. When looked for, much beauty and life can be found in the 'uninteresting' moments. I like to celebrate simplicity, rather than the extravagant, to support the paradigm shift away from the unquenchable thirst for more after more, and instead towards gratitude for what we already own and share. I often struggle with comparing myself to others, and thinking I need more whether that be material, relational, or spiritual. I hope some of my work will encourage both the viewer and myself to look down to the ground we are already standing on and smile. Perhaps some of my other work may help to remind the viewer that they are not alone.

I have explored different lived experiences, such as; battles with mental health, changing direction in life, movements and protests, capturing the pulse of a city, along with slightly more abstract considerations such as the passing of time using light and shadows.

I also love a good DIY project, so indulge me in popping a page up for any pre-loved furniture I've tinkered with to use in my home!

Thank you for taking the time to look around my virtual gallery! Please feel free to get in contact for commissions or any questions.

Peace and love,
